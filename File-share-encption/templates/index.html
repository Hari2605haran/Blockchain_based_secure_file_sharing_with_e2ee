Powerful Features

SecureShare offers everything you need for secure communication and file sharing

End-to-End Encryption

All files and messages are encrypted before they leave your device. Only the intended recipient can decrypt them.

Self-Destructing Files

Set expiration dates for shared files. After the time limit, files are automatically deleted from our servers.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We never have access to your encryption keys or the content of your files. Your data remains private.

Cross-Platform Access

Access your files from any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile. Our apps work seamlessly across platforms.

Large File Support

Share files up to 10GB in size. Perfect for high-resolution videos, design files, and large datasets.

Team Collaboration

Create secure workspaces for your team to share files and communicate with enterprise-grade security.
